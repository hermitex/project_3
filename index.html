Festival Information

My company, "Watch it Outside" just got the approval to show movies in Boston Public Garden this year from August 5th through 8th, from 6pm to midnight. We can't wait to help you & your friends discover awesome films!

Shows will be free and open to everyone, but you're requested to pre-register so that our team can estimate how many people might come and make the appropriate arrangements before hand.
