About Us

Welcome to Watch It Outside, your number one source for the best film event shows. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of films, with a focus on three characteristics, ie: dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2002 by Tiberius, Watch It Outside has come a long way from its beginnings in a toolshed, Houston, TX. When Tiberius first started out, his passion for helping film lovers to enjoy their best fims in a unique and more eco-friendly manner, drove him to research on the best yet simple way to host film events, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to one of the leading film show event agency. We now host our events all over the world, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the entertainment industry. We hope you enjoy our events as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Sincerely, Tiberius, Founder
